# ChaseFree

ChaseFree is a pre-launch accounts receivable platform designed to help agencies and service-based businesses monitor unpaid invoices, identify payment risks, automate customer follow-ups and improve visibility into their cash flow.

🔗 **Live product:** https://chasefree.vercel.app/

## The Problem

Agencies and service businesses often struggle with overdue invoices, inconsistent follow-ups and limited visibility into when payments are likely to arrive.

Manual invoice chasing takes time, creates uncomfortable customer interactions and makes cash-flow planning difficult.

ChaseFree is being built to make accounts receivable management clearer, more organised and less manual.

## Core Product Features

- Invoice monitoring and overdue-payment tracking
- Accounts receivable dashboard
- Payment-risk and collection-probability indicators
- Automated customer follow-up workflows
- Draft review and approval queue
- Recovery analytics and cash-pipeline insights
- Client and invoice management
- Campaign and automation controls
- Accounting and payment integration interfaces
- Billing, team and account-management tools
